Ingredient :
- 8 grams	coffe powder
- 150 ml	fresh water
- 2 spoons	sweetened condensed milk

Directions :
1. Boil the water until 100 Celcius.
2. Remove it from stove, after the buble gone.
3. Put the coffe in cup and pour the water.
4. and last pour the sweetened condensed milk and mix well
5. Ready to be served
